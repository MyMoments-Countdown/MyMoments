Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a new countdown?
Tap the "+" button at the bottom of the Home screen, add a name, select a date, and choose a background.

2. Can I use my own photos?
Yes! Select "Choose from Photos" when creating or editing a moment.

3. How do I enable notifications?
Go to Settings in the app to enable notifications and set a daily reminder.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

At MyMoments, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:
We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details.

Countdown Data:
All countdown data is stored locally on your device.

Photos:
Photos are only used locally for backgrounds and are never uploaded.

2. Third-Party Services

Advertising:
We use services such as Google AdMob, which may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data
  • Approximate location

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your settings.

Apple Analytics:
We may view anonymized data such as download counts.

3. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device.

4. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

5. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
