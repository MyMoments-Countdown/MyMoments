Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a new countdown?

Simply tap the "+" button at the bottom of the Home screen. Give your moment a name, select the date, and choose one of our exclusive tactile backgrounds to match the vibe.

2. Can I use my own photos?

Yes. When creating or editing a moment, tap "Choose from Photos" to personalize your countdown with your own memories.

3. How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings within the app. Here you can toggle notifications on or off and set a specific time for your daily reminder, so you can enjoy the countdown every day.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

At MyMoments, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MyMoments does not collect personal data directly.

Personal Information:
We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or phone number to use the app.

Countdown Data:
All your countdown titles and dates are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal milestones.

Photos:
If you grant the app permission to access your photo library, it is only used to let you select a background for your countdowns. Your photos remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to support and improve the app experience:

Advertising:
MyMoments displays advertisements in the free version of the app using third-party ad providers such as Google AdMob. These services may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Usage data (such as interactions with ads)
  • Approximate location (non-precise)

This data is used to provide and improve advertising. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your device settings and permissions.

Apple App Store Analytics:
We may view anonymous, aggregated data provided by Apple, such as download counts, to understand app performance.

3. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your own device, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings, such as Face ID, Touch ID, or Passcode.

4. Children’s Privacy

MyMoments does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. However, third-party advertising services may collect data as described above.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
